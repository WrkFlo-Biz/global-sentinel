# Oil Shock Cascade Map — $100 WTI

## First-Order Effects (direct)
Oil price up → Energy stocks up, airline stocks down, shipping rates up

## Second-Order Effects (1-4 hours)
- Oil up → China import bill up → Yuan weakens → FXI/KWEB down
- Oil up → Japan import bill up → Nikkei down → Yen up (safe haven)
- Oil up → Fertilizer costs up → Food prices up → EM currencies down
- Oil up → Gas prices up → European industrial costs up → DAX down

## Third-Order Effects (1-3 days)
- Oil up → Inflation expectations up → TIPS up, Gold up
- Oil up → Fed rate cut delayed → Tech stocks pressured
- Oil up → Refining margins widen → MPC/VLO/PSX up
- Oil up → Coal/nuclear demand up → CCJ/UEC up
- Oil up → Insurance premiums up → RNR/MKL up

## Fourth-Order Effects (1-2 weeks)
- Oil up → Consumer spending down → Recession fears → VIX up
- Oil up → Trade deficits widen → EM capital flight
- Oil up → OPEC+ response → Supply dynamics shift
- Oil up → Strategic reserve releases → Political response
